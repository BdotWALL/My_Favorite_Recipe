How To Make These Amazing Sliders

Prep

  1. Cut up the peppers and set aside.
  2. Dice the jalapenos.
  3. Mix the ground turkey in amlarge bowl with the seasonings, egg, bread crumbs, and jalapenos.
  4. Form 12 small patties and set aside.
  5. Open the can of pineapples, drain the juice, cut slices into small wedges and set aside in bowl.

Cooking

  1. In a skillet, griddle, or frying pan of your choice set on medium-high heat, heat the butter and the olive oil
    until the butter has melted and both have coated the pan.
  2. Place 6 of the patties in the pan and cook for about 3-5 minutes per side or until cooked through. Remove and
    cook the 6 remaining patties just like the first. Remove the patties once finished and lower the heat. It is important
    to make sure the patties are cooked thoroughly as this is poultry.
    For more info look here
  3. Add the peppers to the pan and cook for 5 minutes. Remove the peppers from the pan when tender and set aside.
  4. In a smaller frying pan set on medium-high heat, add the pineapple slices and cook for 3-5 minutes. Remove the pan
    from the heat when finished.
  5. Assemble the sliders by first placing the patties on the bottom bun followed by cheese, a drizzle of teriyaki sauce, a
    couple pepper slices, a few diced pineapples, another drizzle of teriyaki sauce, and finally the top bun.
  6. ENJOY!
