The Ingredients For The Sliders

  • 1.5lbs of ground turkey
  • 1 nice sized Jalapeno diced
  • 1 bag of preferred cheese
  • 1 can of pineapple slices or 1 pineapple to be sliced
  • Jar of teriyaki sauce
  • 1 red and 1 orange bell pepper (the smaller the pepper the better)
  • Salt, pepper, and any other preferred seasonings
  • Slider buns
