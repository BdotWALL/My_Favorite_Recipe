Hawaiian Turkey Burger Sliders

This is by far my favorite recipe to make and dish to eat. I love it because the burgers are bursting with so much flavor in the end. The teriyaki sauce
is the perfect pairing for the sweet pineapples on top. The jalapenos are a nice spicy suprise but not over powering. This is quick and easy dish with
minimal prep and cook time which is great especially if you don't want to be in the kitchen that long. These sliders are great for game days, a family gatherings,
parties, or any occasion really.
